Play Pipes

Instructions

HOW TO PLAY:

Connect the pipes from the top left start faucet to the bottom right ending faucet.

Left click to place a pipe on an empty space or to rotate a pipe if a space is occupied.

The pipes will cap themselves off when water fills them up them so no water can pass through them anymore!

Pipes are randomly assigned and you must get create the pipe circuit!

Score is based on how many pipes have water in them at the end of the level.
